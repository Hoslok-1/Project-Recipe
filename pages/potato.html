Hasselback Potatoes Recipe

Fond of potatoes and potato-based dishes? Then you need to try this easy and delicious recipe made with very few ingredients. Hasselback Potatoes are not only pretty to look at but also taste amazing. Packed with garlic, herbs, salt, pepper and butter, these po tatoes are the perfect comfort food. You can make them in just 30 minutes and savour with a creamy dip or ketchup. Do try this recipe, rate it and let us know how it turned out to be.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potato
  • 2 teaspoon butter
  • 6 garlic
  • salt as required
  • black pepper as required

Steps:

  1. Wash the potatoes and pre-heat the oven
  2. Make slits
  3. Stuff garlic
  4. Bake them
  5. Serve hot
